Donate

As a non profit organization we rely on our amazing sponsors and donations to help fund our research. Your donations go towards hardware costs that DriveAI incurs when building self driving cars.

We can engineer the math to solve our hardest problems, generate the code to connect all of our ideas, and ultimately connect steel to steel to make this all a reality. But the only reason, we can do any of this, is because of the trust and support of our generous donors. #Become a part of our story by helping Drive funds to Drive AI.

Contribute to Research

At DriveAI, our research and findings are 100% public. That means anyone can help - and it's easier to get started than you think. Check out our Github, find something that matches your interests or expertise, and have a look at our issues. If you have any questions, shoot us an email at community@driveai.org.

Attend Events

DriveAI hosts events at universities and meeting spaces on a regular basis. If you want to be kept in-the-loop for our future events, join our Meetup group.

Join the Team

If you're as excited about this technology as we are, resolving issues and writing some code won't be enough involvement for you. Feel free to reach out to us at community@driveai.org about making your own research team or joining ours. We look forward to hearing from you!

Community

Welcome to the team! Yes, you read that correctly. We can only do so much as individuals. Progress and innovation requires a collection of bright minds. That's where you come in. How can you provide your expertise? Well, you have a few cool options and certainly aren't limited to one. Engage with your heart's desire.

Attend our Meetup events

We hold them monthly and working to increase the frequency. There you can mingle with other talented people, speak with the DriveAI guys, indulge in some food & drinks, and much more!

Visit our Github Page

Here we have challenges that need to be solved and welcome all to take a stab at it. This is also where you can see a lot of the guts of the operation and what will make this car actually move on its own.

Start a DriveAI Student Org at Your School

This is an opportunity to assemble a squad of your own and work on aspect of the project and encourage the autonomous vehicle space with projects of your own. All you have to do is send us an email at community@driveai.org

If you have any ideas, questions, suggestions, or anything else don't hesitate to reach out to us.

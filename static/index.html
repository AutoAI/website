Contribute

Whether you are an OpenCV wizard, investor, or novice programmer, there are ways for you to contribute. All of our code is completely open sourced and available via Github. We also accept monetary contributions which help fund the sensors, cameras, actuators, processing units and vehicles that we purchase.

Self driving cars are on the way. You can make meaningful contributions now.

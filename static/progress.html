Progress

At DriveAI, we work everyday to make autonomous vehicles a reality. We believe this work should be done in an open way for the benefit of the public. All of our work is freely available under the BSD license - you can view our source code on our GitHub page.

June 2015

DriveAI Platform

This month we're focusing on laying down the groundwork for the DriveAI platform. This is a software package that will integrate into several sensors, and motors to make a car self-driving. We're currently focusing on the portion of the platform that informs the car what the surrounding looks like. With simulated data, we're building out our "Seeded Stereo Correspondence" procedure.
